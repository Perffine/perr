How Victor's Calendar Program! Works

Installation and Setup

Installing the Victor's Calendar Program! extension from the Google Chrome Web Store, puts the necessary code on your computer to enable the extension. To get started, simply click on the icon, then "Click here if below is blank" to initiate the sign-in process.

Sign-In and Authorization

The extension uses a secure sign-in process known as OAuth2 authorization, which is a widely accepted standard for safely granting applications like ours access to your Google Calendar without needing your Google password.

No Data Transmission to Developers

It's important to note that the sign-in process is entirely private â€” no personal data is transmitted to us, the developers, nor is any data stored other than on your local machine.

Accessing Calendar Events

Once signed in, the extension checks for today's and tomorrow's events in your Google Calendar, as well as any events containing the keywords "Doctor," "Dr.," "Appointment," "Appt," or "Birthday." Found events are then neatly displayed in the sidebar of your Chrome browser.

Privacy and Local Processing

We respect your privacy: all data processing happens on your computer, and no calendar data is transmitted to or stored by the extension. Your calendar details remain confidential and are only used to provide you with the highlights within the sidebar.

Easy Access to Your Schedule

By utilizing Victor's Calendar Program!, you can easily keep track of important events without the need to navigate away from your current browser page, ensuring you stay organized effortlessly.
