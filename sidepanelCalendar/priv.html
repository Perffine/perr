Victor's Calendar Program! Privacy Policy

Revised October 24 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the data handling practices of the Victor's Calendar Program! extension. We prioritize the privacy and security of user information.

Information Collection and Use

The extension requests permission to view your Google Calendars names and event data for the sole purpose of displaying calendar highlights within the user interface.

To use Victor's Calendar Program!, you must grant it access to your Google Calendars, and their events. This access is used solely for displaying your calendar highlights within the extension. Victor's Calendar Program! does not store, share, or have access to your personal information, calendar details, or any data related to your Google account.

All data processing occurs locally on your device.

Permissions Required

The extension utilizes the Google Calendar API to function, facilitating the retrieval of calendar names and calendar highlights.

Data Protection

As Victor's Calendar Program! does not store user data, the extension leverages Google's robust security measures to protect your calendar information during API interactions.

Compliance with Laws

We adhere to privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR, ensuring users' rights are respected and protected.

Sharing of Information

No user data is shared with third parties since no data is collected.

User Consent

By utilizing Victor's Calendar Program!, users agree to the processing of their data as described in this policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Users are advised to review this policy periodically. The last update was on October 24 2024.

Authority

User Rights
