Product Specification Sheet
Targus Slimline USB External Floppy Drive
Part #: PA905U
Product Description:
The Slimline USB External Floppy Drive gives you the
convenience of plug-and-play: the first time you insert the
floppy drive, it is automatically recognized and your
notebook will do the rest. Lightweight and compact, this
floppy drive's easy hot plug installation allows you to
connect and disconnect without restarting your notebook.
You can read and write to 3.5” floppy diskettes, including
720K/1.44MB Windows-based format and 1.4MB
Macintosh format. And the drive is conveniently bootable
with most notebook computers.
Compatible with Windows® 2000, ME, XP and Mac
OS 9 and higher.
